Only `MANUAL_X_REF` rows carry the source's own accession. Rows of other
types under the same source_id hold a different identifier entirely -- a CAS registry
number, a citation -- and must not be read as accessions. Of those, only rows whose
curation status is one of ['CHECKED', 'OK'] are taken; SUBMITTED rows
are a depositor's unreviewed claim (issue #957).
